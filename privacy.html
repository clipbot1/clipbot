Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2025

Clip Bot (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it:

1. Data We Collect

  • TikTok Profile Info: via Login Kit (user ID, display name, avatar).
  • Generated Clips: Temporarily stored to upload to TikTok, then deleted.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate with TikTok and post clips on your behalf.
  • To troubleshoot errors (we log upload successes/failures internally).

3. Data Retention

We keep your profile info until you revoke access. Clip files are deleted immediately after posting.

4. Contact

If you have privacy questions, email support@clipbot.example.com.
